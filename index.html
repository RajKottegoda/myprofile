About Me

I am Rajitha Kottegoda. I am a creative, energetic and hardworking person. I am able to handle multiple tasks individually or with my teammates. I am a honest and trustworthy person who is great at time management as well.
I am an undergraduate of university of Sri Jayewardenepura. I went to Prince of Wales College. Then I have done O/L's in T/Kan/Mollipothana M.V. and I have done my A/L's in T/Kan/Ganthalawa M.V.
I am a freelance graphic and UI/UX designer and also I am working on video editing (footages and animations) too. And also I am a article writer, poet and a lyricist. I spend most of my free time to read books.
The best thing of being a graphic designer is I can use my own creativity to make someone's imagination real. So, That's me.
